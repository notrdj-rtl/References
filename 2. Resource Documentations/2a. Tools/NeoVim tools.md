### KeyBinds

Meta+alt+arrow = **move windows**
esc u = **undo**
i = **edit**
a = **append** 
:q =  **quit nvim**
:wq = **quit with changes**
dw = **delete word**
d$ = **Delete sentence**

---

GNOME useful keybinds:

Switch: super f/j OR super arrows
take window: super t
resize: super +/-
make right of window below current: i/o
fullscreen: super shift f
obsidian switch tabs: ctrl left arrow / right arrow
workspace change: super above-tab
